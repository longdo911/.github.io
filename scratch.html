Here is a game I made! Totoro

Totoro is the title character in the Japanese animated film by Studio Ghibli 'My Neighbor Totoro'. He is a fuzzy, giant, furry, pointy-eared creature with wide eyes and a fantastic smile. In this game, totoro is trapped inside a box and controlled by an evil spirit and trying to kill you! There are three bosses: mini totoro, medium totoro and totoro, and the challenge of this game is to dodge every single projectile the boss shoots at you, as a cat bus, until he gets out of energy and fades away! GLHF~
